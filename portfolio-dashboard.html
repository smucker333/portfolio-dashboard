Connect your Google Sheet

Follow these steps once, then your dashboard will always pull live data from your sheet.

Step 1: In your Google Sheet, click Extensions → Apps Script
Step 2: Delete any existing code, then paste in the Code.gs file provided with this dashboard
Step 3: Click Deploy → New deployment → Web app. Set "Who has access" to Anyone. Click Deploy and copy the URL
Step 4: Paste that URL below and click Save
